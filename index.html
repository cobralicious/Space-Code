Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most frequently asked questions about our courses

Do I need programming experience to start learning?

+

No, most of our courses are designed for beginners. We start with the basics and gradually move on to more complex topics. The main thing is the desire to learn and develop!

How much time should you devote to learning?

+

We recommend devoting at least 10-15 hours a week to studying. With intensive study (20+ hours a week), you can master the program faster.

Is it possible to pay in installments?

+

Yes, we offer installments up to 12 months without interest. Student discounts and preferential programs are also available.

Do you help with employment?

+

Yes, we have a career center that helps with resume writing, interview preparation, and job searches. We work with 500+ IT companies.

Is it possible to learn at your own pace?

+

Of course! All materials are available 24/7, you can study them at your own pace. There are both independent and group learning formats.
